Recording

Basement Jams

The earliest recording I have date back to the Teal Steel days, but mostly began during COVID, when I had a lot of time to experiment freely with the microphones and cheap recording gear my dad bought me.

NYU

The big draw for me attending NYU in New York was the ability to use their recording studio facilities, which are some of the finest for a university and finest in the world. The number of hours I have spent recording in them so far is easily in the triple digits and I hope to record countless more songs and sessions until I graduate.
